About Us

BabyChime™ brings you beautiful and original sterling silver products for expecting moms and babies. We take great pride in offering you quality products at small prices.

BabyChime is a family-owned business and the idea was born during our first pregnancy. A friend in Europe sent us a special gift: a beautiful chime with a soothing sound that could promote bonding with your baby and that she would even recognize after birth as a calming influence. The chime brought us hours of joy, bonding, and happiness and we decided to found BabyChime in the hope they would one day do the same for others in the United States.

We began work with a group of silversmiths to create our own designs of these functional, special, and timeless momentos of pregnancy - BabyChime was created!

We invite you to view our unique and stylish chimes and hope that our BabyChimes bring a bit of peace and comfort to your pregnancy and put a smile on your face.... and your baby's!
